Bio — one line

Nadia Pomirleanu, Ph.D., is a Professor of Marketing and researcher on experiential services, sensorial marketing, and the Las Vegas experience economy.

Bio — short

Nadia Pomirleanu, Ph.D., is Professor of Marketing and Mel Larson Fellow of Experiential Marketing at UNLV's Lee Business School. Her research on services, experiential marketing, and B2B strategy has appeared in the Journal of Retailing, Journal of Business Research, and Industrial Marketing Management, and she advises museums, cultural institutions, and operators in hospitality, sports, and entertainment.

Experiential and immersive services · sensory design in venues, museums, and attractions · arts and culture marketing and audience development · customer experience and loyalty · Las Vegas hospitality, sports, and entertainment · consumer pricing, fees, and fairness · B2B relationships and sales strategy.
